What should you discard from hand B? If you wanted to make a hand like A, you should discard u or c. There's not much difference between the two, however since a wait closer to the edge is better, it's better to discard c to make the zv wait more likely.

How about C? Some people might want to keep all of their ryanmen shapes and discard c. Let's think about a slightly more complicated situation with a potential iipeikou. Hand E has three pairs, so let's break up one of the pairs in the pinzu or souzu. Usually you would want to discard k to maximize your iipeikou potential. But to prioritize speed, you could also discard c: there's more of zv than fj, not to mention zv being easier to come out.[2] Hand F has two pairs, so you want to confirm one of them as a pair. Usually you would discard x for iipeikou, but when prioritizing speed you would discard h.

Regardless, remember that when your hand looks close to a perfect 1-shanten, you should keep two pairs in your hand.
