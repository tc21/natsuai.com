What would you discard from this hand? Don't think too hard about it. Which tile was your first instinct?

  1. zxcvvb
  2. zxxcvb
  3. xxcvvb

The only suit you can discard from is pinzu. Think about what the pinzu shape is. Most people's first instinct is to see this as zxc + vvb, and discard x. The resulting shape is A. The cn-ryanmen, and v4 can get you to tenpai. This is a “perfect 1-shanten” shape. Some people might want to secure the vb-ryanmen first, discarding v. The resulting shape is B. x also gets you tenpai in this shape.

Another potential discard is z. The resulting shape is C. You can use cn and x, just like B.

In other words, A, B, and C have the same efficiency. Clearly, C is the best shape here as it keeps the iipeikou potential, and z is the best discard.

Although z is the best discard here, it seems that many people don't even realize that z is an option. I asked many people around me, and most of them saw the hand as zxc + vvb, and are not familiar with the shape in C. Many of them are slow to understand similar shapes too, such as rrtyyu or ghhjkk.

  1. zxcvvbb

If you can quickly understand and choose between the shapes A, B, and C, you will be able to have more choices when you play mahjong. For example, if your were to choose two tiles to discard from D, of course if you were thinking about tanyao-pinfu-iipeikou you would discard zx. But when thinking about tile safety or ease of calling tiles, vb, zb, or bz are also possible orders of discarding tiles.

Improve your familiarity with different shapes. Then you will be able to make the best decision depending on the situation you're in.
