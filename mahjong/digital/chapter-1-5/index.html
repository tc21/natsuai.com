First let's look and hand A. If you discard 4, what tiles can you draw that will put you into tenpai? vm and gk are very obvious. But if you treat the pinzu shape as zcb + vbn, you can see that x is also a tenpai draw. You want to not miss this, and play 4 in this situation. If you intend to keep 4 as a safe tile, understand that you are sacrificing the speed of your hand by losing the x draw.

This kind of shape is called “ryanmen-kanchan”. This shape occurs frequently in mahjong, and people often miss it. Especially when you have a hand like A, you might only see the tanyao and accidentally think that z is useless. In a shape like hand B where you can see a sanshoku, most people probably wouldn't be discarding z.
