East round, and points are fairly level (we started with 30000 points in this game). The turn before, I discarded ., not to be focused on honiitsu. That said, the hand naturally become honiitsu if I draw a middle pinzu tile. I then called the 6 that was discarded immediately after. What should I discard here? Looking for honiitsu or honroutou, 3 and x should be kept. So I'm choosing between t and d. Considering that two s have already been discarded, some people would discard d, but is that really the best choice?

Discarding s this early on is a sign that someone doesn't have a to d, and also can't use a and s. With two people discarding s, waiting on tiles next to d would be really good. What about tiles next to t? Although they have not been discarded, we cannot guarantee they still remain in the wall. Considering that e is the dora, we cannot necessarily hope that someone will deal into us.

In this game, I chose to discard t. Even if I can't use the dora anymore, I can get chanta by drawing souzu. Even without that, this hand is valuable enough with the riichi sticks + 3 honba.
